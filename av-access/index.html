Challenges to Automated Access to Video in the DPLA

  • Not able to store derivatives locally
  • No consistent direct URL to video resources in metadata
  • Diversity of delivery platforms at host sites

Looking Ahead

  • Distributed, not centralized
  • Make video collections more accessible to machines from where they already are
  • Allow services from outside to connect to them
Problem established, but where to from here??

Solutions

  • Open standards (e.g., HTML 5 <video> element, schema.org VideoObject properties )
  • Provide URL to file in metadata/API
Some basic (but not easy/scalable) solutions... - in browser, schema.org could be devoted to more active consumption. - of course need another standard for sharing video accross collections (exists?)
